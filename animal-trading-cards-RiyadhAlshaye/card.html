Camel

With the hump that stores up to 80 lb of fats. Camels can travel up to 100 desert mile without drinking water

  • Scientific Name: Camelus dromedarius
  • Size: Over 7 feets tall
  • Weight: Up to 1,600 lb
  • Diet: Herbivore (plants)

It is known as Arabian Camels. It rarely sweat even in the desert where the temperture reaches up to 120F,which reduce the fluid they need. Camels have big and thick footbads which help them walk through the sand and rocks.
